King Island Meat Providore

We are locally owned and operated by five King Island families with origins as beef & sheep farmers and food & meat connoisseurs. We might be biased but we believe King Island produces some of the best beef & lamb in the world.

King Island is renowned for it's impeccible growing conditions, Pure air and water, Green grassy pastures, and a hint of sea salt. Cattle and sheep roam freely, creating the perfect low stress enviroment.
