We are locally owned and operated by five King Island families with origins as beef & sheep farmers and food & meat connoisseurs. We might be biased but we believe King Island produces some of the best beef & lamb in the world.

King Island is renowned for it's impeccable growing conditions, Pure air and water, Green grassy pastures, and a hint of sea salt. Cattle and sheep roam freely, creating the perfect low stress enviroment.

Our meat is free of hormones, antibiotics and GMOs. The low stress enviroment guarantees the most tender, juicy and flavoursome beef and lamb.
